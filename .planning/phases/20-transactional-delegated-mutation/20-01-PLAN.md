<objective>
Create the durable transaction record that every later gate, merge, rollback, and cleanup step can trust.

Purpose: F20 needs one replayable lifecycle instead of ephemeral child-agent success claims or caller-supplied snapshots. This plan opens the transaction under retained journal authority and turns the accepted `wcore-types` receipt contract into durable `wcore-agent` state.
Output: An opaque authoritative opening token, store API, journal/reducer support, and hostile persistence tests.
</objective>
